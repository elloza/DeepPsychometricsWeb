AEMCCO XVI Congress on Social and Health Sciences Methodology

Thursday, Jul 11, 2019| Tags: Conference, inspiration

On 8 and 10 July 2019, the XVI Congress on Social and Health Sciences Methodology was held at the Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (UAM).

This event is held biannually, under the auspices of the Spanish Association of Methodology of Behavioral Sciences (AEMCCO).

Its aim is to encourage and promote the exchange of knowledge between teachers, researchers, professionals and students interested in methodological issues in the field of Social Sciences, Education, Health and Behavioural Sciences.

The preliminary results of the work have been presented at this congress: “Neural network models and response biases”.

A great day and accompanied by great researchers. Thank you to all the assistants!
